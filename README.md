**Project Title**
**SQL Business Analytics**

**Project Overview**

This project uses the Northwind database to perform sales and customer analysis using MySQL. The database contains information about customers, orders, products, and order details, providing a realistic business environment for SQL-based data analysis. The project demonstrates the use of SQL concepts such as JOINs, aggregate functions, GROUP BY, subqueries, and window functions to retrieve, analyze, and summarize business data.
The analysis focuses on generating meaningful business reports, including Monthly Revenue Trends, Top Products by Revenue, Customer Segmentation, and Top Customers by Revenue. Query optimization techniques such as indexing, query restructuring, and execution plan analysis were also applied to improve performance. The insights derived from these reports help understand sales patterns, customer behavior, and product performance, enabling data-driven decision-making.

**Tools**
MySQL Server 8.0
MySQL Workbench
Northwind Sample Database

**Working**
1. Created and configured the Northwind database in MySQL.
2. Imported and organized customer, order, product, and sales data into relational tables.
3. Performed data analysis using SQL queries, including JOINs, aggregate functions, GROUP BY, subqueries, and window functions.
4. Generated business reports such as Monthly Revenue Trends, Top Products, Customer Segmentation, and Top Customers.
5. Optimized query performance using indexing and query restructuring techniques.
6. Interpreted the results to derive business insights and support data-driven decision-making.

**Business Intrepretation**
**Top 10 Products by Revenue**

* The report identifies the products generating the highest revenue for the business.
* High-revenue products contribute significantly to overall sales performance.
* These products can be prioritized for marketing, promotions, and inventory management.
* Understanding top-performing products helps improve profitability and business growth.

**Customer Segmentation**

* Customers are classified into High Value, Medium Value, and Low Value segments based on their total spending.
* High Value customers contribute the largest share of revenue and should be retained through loyalty programs.
* Medium Value customers have potential to move into the High Value segment with targeted offers.
* Low Value customers represent occasional buyers and may require engagement strategies to increase purchases.

**Customer-wise Highest Revenue**

* The report identifies customers who generate the highest revenue for the business.
* Top customers are key contributors to company sales and profitability.
* Maintaining strong relationships with these customers can improve customer retention.
* The report helps management focus on valuable customers and design personalized business strategies.

**Conclusion**
This project used SQL queries to analyze sales data from the Northwind database. By generating reports on revenue trends, top products, and customer behavior, valuable business insights were obtained. The results demonstrate how SQL can support data-driven decision-making and improve overall business performance.
